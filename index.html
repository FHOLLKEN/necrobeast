Bio

Twenty years ago, in the deep south of Chile, Necrobeast was born. As a one-man project, calling forth the Ancient One amidst black skies and endless rain, Necrobeast brings the raw power of old school black thrash metal to the world. Their lyrics delve into the nature of the cosmos, the depths of the human soul, the endless void of hell, and the ultimate war between the celestial powers. Necrobeast music is inspired by South American, German, and Japanese thrash metal (Sabbat, Sarcófago, Atomic Aggressor, Desaster) and Nordic Black Metal (Bathory, Dissection, early Darkthrone), creating brutal, neck-breaking war cries.

Currently, Necrobeast is based in Maastricht (Netherlands), where its full-length ‘Iron Baphomet’ has been brought to life. Iron Baphomet is eight songs of pure raw black thrash onslaught, recorded in the dwelling of the gods, Uppsala. In 2025, its cult demo ‘El despertar de la Necrobestia’ (2006) has been reissued by Brutal Insanity Records (Netherlands) on tape format.
